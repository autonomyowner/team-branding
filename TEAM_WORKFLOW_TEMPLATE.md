## Handoff Processes

### Tech to Sales (Azeddine -> Meamar)
1. Product completion notification
2. Technical documentation provided
3. Demo/walkthrough session
4. Sales materials prepared
5. Listing creation

### Content to Tech (Sohir/Hythem/Meamar -> Azeddine)
1. Brand assets delivered
2. Copy and text content provided (Meamar - sales copy)
3. Review and feedback cycle
4. Final approval

### Branding to Sales (Meamar internal workflow)
1. Brand positioning finalized
2. Marketing materials created
3. Sales copy for Flippa listings
4. Product pitch deck ready
